Stories of Excellence

Helping to build the future of the internet

ASU is collaborating with Cisco, the Oak Ridge National Laboratory and others to design a Quantum Networking Lab to create computer systems that deliver information faster and more securely.

Innovating to make college more accessible

Thanks to a new collaboration between ASU, YouTube and Crash Course, students can take college courses on YouTube and earn low-cost credit that transfers to hundreds of colleges and universities.

New partnership with Clean-Seas to create plastic-to-hydrogen facility

The first-of-its-kind hydrogen facility will convert plastic waste into clean hydrogen to bring sustainable energy to Arizona.

Engineering a better world

Students in ASU’s Engineers Without Borders are receiving mentorship and applying the skills they’re learning to projects that are positively impacting people around the world.

ASU professor using X-rays to discover new knowledge

The director of an unprecedented ASU lab aims to use X-ray technology to advance scientific efforts in areas such as drug development and renewable energy.

Hollywood-style technology comes to the classroom

A revolutionary lab at ASU’s Thunderbird School of Global Management is using holograms and augmented reality to advance the way students learn.
